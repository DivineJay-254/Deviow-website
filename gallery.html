Community highlights

Livelihoods, healing, advocacy, and play

Each image represents the dedication of volunteers, mentors, and participants who make DEVIOW's mission possible.

Community Awareness Campaign

Women, men, and community leaders gather for a GBV awareness campaign and community mobilization session in Kakuma.

Tailoring Skills Training

Women learn tailoring and dressmaking skills in a structured workshop, gaining vocational training to build sustainable livelihoods.

Practical Sewing Skills Development

Participants practice hands-on tailoring techniques, developing professional skills that enable them to start microenterprises and earn income.

Mentorship in Vocational Training

An experienced DEVIOW mentor guides women through tailoring curriculum, providing personalized support and knowledge transfer for skill mastery.

Intergenerational Skills Transfer

Young girls receive tailoring training alongside adult women, ensuring youth engagement and building a pipeline of skilled entrepreneurs.

DEVIOW Leadership & Community

The DEVIOW team and community leaders stand united, representing the refugee-led vision of empowerment and solidarity in defying violence against women.
