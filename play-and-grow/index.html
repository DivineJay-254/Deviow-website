Play & Grow

Promoting safe, nurturing spaces for children

The Play & Grow Initiative integrates play-based learning, creative arts, and storytelling to support children’s social and emotional development in Kakuma and Kalobeyei.

Why Play Matters

Recognizing that healing and empowerment start early, Play & Grow helps children express emotions, build empathy, and learn equality through guided play. Parents, caregivers, and educators join us to cultivate safe and inclusive environments.

  • Creative arts, music, and storytelling that nurture resilience.
  • Child protection protocols plus snacks and hygiene kits.
  • Caregiver sessions on positive discipline and wellbeing.

40+ children engaged weekly

Components

Holistic care for children and caregivers

Play Therapy

Facilitators trained in trauma-informed play guide art, music, and movement sessions that encourage self-expression.

Literacy Pods

Reading corners stocked with multilingual books and games reinforce early learning skills for ages 4-12.

Caregiver Circles

Parents and guardians attend circles on child protection, equality, and positive discipline.

Safe Space Kits

Each child receives art supplies, notebooks, and hygiene items to continue learning at home.

Our Impact

The initiative has inspired teachers and caregivers to use play as a tool for growth and inclusion in local learning centers.
