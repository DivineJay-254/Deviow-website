In Kakuma and Kalobeyei

Empowering Women and Girls Through Skills, Protection, and Opportunity

In Kakuma, many women and girls carry responsibilities far beyond their age and resources. Some are survivors of violence. Some are teenage mothers. Others simply never had the opportunity to learn a skill that could support daily survival.

PRODEVIOW exists to respond to these realities with practical support through tailoring training, financial literacy, protection awareness, and community support programs that help restore confidence, dignity, and self-reliance. Our work is community-driven, human-centered, and focused on long-term impact.
