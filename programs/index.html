Our Programs

Holistic support for dignity and self-reliance

DEVIOW integrates livelihood empowerment, financial inclusion, GBV prevention, and child wellbeing so every woman and girl can thrive.

Skills & Tailoring Empowerment Program

Economic independence through practical skills

Many women in Kakuma have limited access to employment opportunities. Through tailoring and dressmaking training, we help participants gain practical skills that can become a source of income.

Participants learn:

  • Basic and advanced tailoring
  • Garment cutting and stitching
  • Repair and alteration work
  • Customer handling and pricing
  • Basic entrepreneurship skills

The goal is not only training. The goal is economic independence.

Protection & GBV Awareness Program

Safe spaces and community strength

Community Awareness Dialogue

Women and girls continue to face different forms of violence, discrimination, and social exclusion. This program focuses on creating safety and building community accountability.

Program activities include:

  • GBV awareness sessions
  • Safe spaces for women and girls
  • Referral pathways for survivors
  • Community sensitization
  • Promoting dignity and protection

We work to strengthen awareness while encouraging communities to become part of the solution.

Financial Literacy & VSLA Support

Building financial resilience together

Income without financial planning often remains unstable. Through financial literacy and Village Savings and Loan Association (VSLA) support, women learn to manage money strategically.

Women learn:

  • Saving practices and financial discipline
  • Budgeting and expense tracking
  • Loan management and repayment
  • Small business planning
  • Group financial accountability

The program helps participants build resilience and reduce dependency.

Play & Grow Program

Safe, nurturing spaces for children

Play & Grow Community Session

Children both girls and boys often face isolation, stress, and limited opportunities for personal growth. Using gamified sports and creative activities, this program creates supportive spaces where participants can thrive.

Participants engage in:

  • Confidence building activities
  • Life skills and social development
  • Mentorship activities
  • Sports and creative play
  • Social and emotional development

We believe growth is not only academic or financial. Emotional wellbeing also matters.
